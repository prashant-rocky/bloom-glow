Garden Maintenance Packages.

A beautiful garden requires ongoing care to stay healthy, vibrant, and productive. At Bloom Glow, our Garden Maintenance Packages are designed to take the stress out of garden upkeep while ensuring your outdoor space continues to thrive all year round. From weekly touch-ups to monthly care plans, we offer flexible solutions that suit every home and lifestyle.

Comprehensive Care for Every Garden

Our maintenance packages cover everything your garden needs — seasonal planting, pruning, watering, and soil management. We also handle pest and weed control using eco-friendly, safe methods to protect your plants while keeping your garden sustainable. With our team taking care of the details, your garden remains a flourishing, worry-free retreat.

Whether you prefer a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly service, we customize our schedule to match your garden’s requirements and your lifestyle. Our team monitors plant health, adjusts care routines for each season, and ensures your garden looks its best all year long.

What We Provide

  • Regular Care Plans: Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits tailored to your garden’s needs.
  • Seasonal Planting: Planting flowers, vegetables, and herbs according to the season for year-round beauty and productivity.
  • Pruning & Trimming: Expert trimming of plants, shrubs, and small trees to maintain shape and health.
  • Pest & Weed Management: Eco-friendly solutions to prevent pests and weeds without harming your plants.

Senior-Friendly & Stress-Free

For seniors or busy homeowners, garden maintenance can be a challenge. Bloom Glow provides an easy, hands-off solution that keeps your garden thriving without heavy lifting or constant attention. You can enjoy the beauty and benefits of a well-maintained garden without the physical strain.

Beyond physical maintenance, we provide guidance on seasonal planting, plant care, and sustainable gardening tips. Our experts help you make informed decisions to keep your garden productive and visually stunning.
